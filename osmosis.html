Osmosis Marketing
    At the age of 14, created a small retail business go gain additional funds. Later on, I revamped the company into a marketing company after finding an advertisement flyer full with grammatical and spelling errors. Realizing that I could do a much better job at creating and also distributing flyers, I created Osmosis Marketing. Within a year, I managed to gather several employees to distribute and create advertisements for clients. I also expanded into social marketing and creating websites for clients, providing a suitable representation on the internet for the respective client.
